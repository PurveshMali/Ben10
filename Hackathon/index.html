Ben 10 Timeline

Discovery of the Omnitrix

During a summer road trip with his cousin Gwen and Grandpa Max, Ben discovers the mysterious Omnitrix, a powerful alien device that changes his life forever.

First Transformation into Heatblast

Ben accidentally activates the Omnitrix, transforming into Heatblast for the first time. He learns about his newfound powers while saving a nearby forest from a fire.

Battle with Vilgax

Ben faces Vilgax, a powerful alien warlord who wants the Omnitrix for himself. With the help of his family, Ben learns to harness his alien forms to defeat this formidable enemy.
